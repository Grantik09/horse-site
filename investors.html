Penglai Performance Mechanics & Next Gen DriveTech Corporation Ltd

Global Mobility Innovator

PPMDTC was formed in 2008 to consolidate the high-performance engineering, smart vehicle systems, and AI-powered mobility platforms of Penglai Performance Mechanics LTD—a global leader in next-gen transport tech. PPMDTC delivers cutting-edge electric drivetrains, wireless telemetry systems, and smart helmet communication solutions across 20 countries and 3 continents.

Tenderloin Capital

Venture Capital Firm

What makes us top gear and not your typical VC? Backed by over three decades of combined industry and startup experience, Tenderloin Capital was founded to support bold creators revving up the future of mobility. We’re more than just capital—our team rides alongside founders as real partners. Tenderloin backs entrepreneurs building engine-intelligent, route-optimized, hyper-local, two-wheeled experiences that disrupt markets and fuel change.

The Garage

Accelerator

The Garage is a hub for tuning more than just engines—it fuels the future of smart mobility. While originally focused on restoring vintage café racers, The Garage now accelerates the most daring ventures in EV tech, modular design, and urban ride-sharing ecosystems. As pioneers of sustainable, human-centric, and fully-customized transport, The Garage empowers early-stage motorbike innovators with tools, mentorship, and serious torque.
