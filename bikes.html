Wild Rydes has a dedicated staff that recruits, trains, and tends to our herd of bikes. We take great pride in the quality of bikes and rydes that we provide to our customers, and our staff exercises the utmost care in vetting the bikes that join our herd.

Every bike goes through a rigorous due diligence process where we perform background checks, flying exams, and several rounds of interviews. Bikes accepted to Wild Rydes are then treated to the best care and maintenance possible. We provide them excellent benefits, health care, and employee perks. This is part of our company philosophy in which happy bikes lead to happy customers.

Meet a few of the bikes that are part of our family.

Indian Motorcycle

Indian Scout Bobber

Indian Scout Bobber rolled into Wild Rydes in the summer of 2018 and instantly became a fan favorite. With her low-slung stance and raw attitude, she brings an old-school cool to every ride. She’s known for spontaneous road trips, vintage leather jackets, and always having the best playlist blasting through her Bluetooth exhaust.

Yamaha MT-09

Kawasaki Ninja 400

Yamaha MT-09 joined Wild Rydes after a celebrated run as a show bike in motorsport expos around the world. With his responsive throttle and bold styling, he loves dazzling riders with a mix of performance and personality. He’s also known to bust out a burnout or two when no one’s looking.

Royal Enfield Classic 350

Triumph Bonneville Bobber

BMW R 1250 GS recently joined the Wild Rydes team in Madrid, Spain. She helped launch Wild Rydes’ European expansion after a legendary career as an adventure tourer. She thrives on long journeys, rugged trails, and documenting her travels on BikeTok.
